About Me

Hi! I'm Nandita, a current undergraduate at Stanford studying computer science and creative writing. My poetry and prose has been published in literary journals, recognized by the UK Poetry Society, the Keats-Shelley Prizes, and the Alliance for Young Artists and Writers, and nominated for the Best of the Net.
